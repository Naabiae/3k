FAQ

Quick answers for real users.

What do I share with someone who wants to pay me?

Share your email pay link. The backend resolves it to your router address, and the app can generate a QR payload.

Where does the “savings” money go?

It’s deposited into an ERC‑4626 vault and represented as onchain shares owned by your router. You can withdraw later.

Can I change the split later?

Yes. The router has settings for operating, treasury, locked, and savings percent. The app UI is prepared for this flow.
